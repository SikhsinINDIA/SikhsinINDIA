Gurudwara Sahib Ji (Armane Nagar) - Bangalore

Gurudwara Sahib in Armane Nagar exudes divine tranquillity with its majestic architecture and serene ambience. It serves as a spiritual oasis for devotees, offering prayers and seeking blessings in a peaceful environment.

Described by visitors as a “beautiful Gurudwara… a place where one can understand the meaning of peace and tranquility”

Address: G2nd Cross Road, 1st Block, RMV 2nd Stage, Ashwath Nagar, Armane Nagar, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560006, India
