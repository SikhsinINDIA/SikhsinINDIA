Gurudwara Sahib Ejipura Cantonment - Bangalore

Gurudwara Sahib Ejipura Cantonment is renowned for its magnificent architecture and tranquil surroundings. It serves as a spiritual retreat for devotees seeking inner peace and divine blessings.

Gurudwara Sahib Eejipura Cantonment stands as a beacon of peace and spirituality in the bustling city of Bengaluru. This Sikh temple is renowned not only for its stunning architecture but also for its welcoming atmosphere that draws visitors from all walks of life.

As you step inside, you are enveloped by a sense of tranquility, with the sound of hymns and the sight of devotees engaged in prayer creating a deeply spiritual ambiance. The intricate details of the Gurudwara’s design reflect the rich cultural heritage of Sikhism, making it a visual delight for tourists and photographers alike.

Address: Agram, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560007, India
