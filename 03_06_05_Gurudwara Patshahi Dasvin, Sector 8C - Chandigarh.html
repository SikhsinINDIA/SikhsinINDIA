Gurudwara Patshahi Dasvin, Sector 8C - Chandigarh

This shrine commemorates a visit by Guru Gobind Singh Ji, the Tenth Sikh Guru, during his travels through the region. It is one of Chandigarh's key historical gurudwaras.

Built as a tribute to Guru Gobind Singh Ji, the gurudwara serves as a spiritual landmark dedicated to his legacy.

The gurudwara features a serene and beautifully planned layout with a white and blue exterior, well maintained green spaces, and flowering gardens that add to its peaceful atmosphere

Address: 1101, Madhya Marg, Sector 8C, Chandigarh – 160008
