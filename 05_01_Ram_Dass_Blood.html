Inspired by Sikh values • Serving Humanity since 2008

About Us

Founded in 2008 and registered in July 2009, the Shri Guru Ram Dass Blood Donation Sewa Society is a non-profit volunteer-based NGO operating out of Amritsar, Punjab. It honors the legacy of Guru Ram Dass Ji, the 4th Sikh Guru, by promoting voluntary blood donation and broader social reform.

Core Team: 25 members (11 executives + 14 volunteers)

  • President: Sukhwinder Singh
  • Advisor: Maninder Singh Simran

Mission & Activities

  • Promote voluntary blood donation across communities
  • Organize donation camps and support government blood banks
  • Mobilize emergency donors quickly
  • Assist underprivileged patients financially (tests, medicines)
  • Fight social evils: dowry, substance abuse, female foeticide
  • Run cultural/social programs and disaster relief services

Contributions

  • Maintain a ready pool of blood donors
  • Provide blood and monetary aid to those in need
